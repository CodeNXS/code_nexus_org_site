About Code Nexus

Our Story

Born in the vibrant tech ecosystem of Singapore, Code Nexus emerged from a simple yet powerful belief: technology should serve everyone, not just those who can afford it. What started as a group of passionate students wanting to give back has evolved into an international movement of young technologists dedicated to social impact.

We bridge the digital divide by connecting skilled student developers with organizations that need technology the most—from local NGOs fighting poverty to small businesses trying to establish their digital presence.

Mission

To democratize access to technology tools and learning opportunities, ensuring that financial barriers never prevent innovation or growth.

Vision

To build the next generation of socially-conscious tech creators who use their skills to solve real-world problems and create positive change.

Our Values

Accessibility

Technology should be available to everyone, regardless of background or resources.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of working together to achieve greater impact.

Innovation

We embrace creative solutions and cutting-edge approaches to social challenges.

Impact

Everything we do is measured by the positive change it creates in communities.

Dream Project

Singapore Teen Hackathon

A free, national-level hackathon for all teenagers in Singapore

The Vision

We're building Singapore's premier hackathon exclusively for teenagers aged 13-18, focused on solving real-world problems that matter to young people. This isn't just another coding competition—it's a platform for the next generation to showcase their creativity, innovation, and passion for positive change.

Who Can Participate

Any teenager in Singapore aged 13-18, regardless of coding experience. We welcome beginners, experts, and everyone in between.

Problem Areas

Climate change, education inequality, mental health, urban sustainability, and social justice—issues that define our generation.

Expert Mentorship

Direct access to industry professionals, startup founders, and tech leaders who will guide participants throughout the event.

Recognition & Prizes

Winning teams receive not just prizes, but opportunities for internships, mentorship, and support to develop their ideas further.

2025 Pilot Program

We're launching our pilot program in late 2025 with 100 participants. This will be the foundation for what we hope becomes Singapore's most anticipated annual teen tech event.
